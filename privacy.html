3. What personal information we collect

We design this site to collect as little personal information as reasonably possible. We do not require you to register an account. However, we may process the following categories of personal information:

  • Analytics information – when you accept analytics cookies, Google Analytics collects aggregated data about how you use the site (for example, which pages you visit and how long you stay on them).
  • Communication information – any information you choose to provide when you contact us directly (for example by email).

4. How we collect personal information

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • Directly from you – for example, when you submit a comment or contact us.
  • Automatically – through the use of cookies, server logs, and similar technologies when you browse the site.
  • Through analytics tools – when you consent to analytics cookies, Google Analytics collects information about how you interact with the site.

5. Why we use your personal information (purposes)

We use personal information only where necessary for specific, lawful purposes, including to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website and its content.
  • Display, moderate, and manage public comments and user-generated content.
  • Protect the site against spam, abuse, fraud, and security threats.
  • Generate aggregated analytics reports to understand site performance and usage (where you have consented to analytics cookies).
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities.
  • Manage and resolve queries, complaints, or disputes.

6. Lawful grounds for processing under POPIA

We process personal information on one or more of the following grounds recognised by POPIA:

  • Consent – for example, where you accept analytics cookies via the cookie banner.
  • Legitimate interests – for example, operating and improving the website, moderating comments, ensuring security, and preventing misuse, where these interests are not overridden by your privacy rights.
  • Legal obligations – for example, complying with record-keeping, regulatory, or court requirements.
  • Limited processing for the legitimate interests of you or GoNoGo – for example, in relation to security and fraud prevention.

Where we rely on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time using the mechanisms described in this policy. Withdrawal will not affect the lawfulness of processing before consent was withdrawn.

7. Comments and public content

If you submit a comment on the site, the name or display name you provide and the content of your comment may be published publicly on the relevant page.

You should not include sensitive personal information, confidential information, or information about other people in your comments unless you are legally entitled to do so. We operate moderation and may review, edit, or remove comments where necessary to comply with law or protect others.

8. Cookies, analytics, and similar technologies

The site uses cookies and similar technologies to function and, where you consent, to understand how visitors use the site.

  • Strictly necessary cookies – required for basic operation of the site and to remember your cookie preferences.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) – used to collect aggregated information about site usage, such as pages visited and time spent on the site. These only operate if you click "Accept" in the cookie banner.

Analytics cookies are not set until you provide consent. You can change your cookie choice at any time via the cookie banner controls (where provided) or through your browser settings. Further details are available in our Cookie Policy.

These providers may process personal information on our behalf as "operators" under POPIA. They may also process information outside South Africa (for example, in the United Kingdom, European Union, or other regions where their systems are located).

We take reasonable steps to ensure that when personal information is transferred outside South Africa, it is protected by adequate safeguards in line with POPIA, such as contractual protections, appropriate technical and organisational measures, or the presence of adequate data protection laws in the destination country.

10. How long we keep personal information

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

  • Comments and related moderation records may be retained while the comment remains publicly visible and for a limited period afterwards.
  • Technical and analytics data are retained in line with system settings and Google Analytics data retention options, subject to periodic review.
  • Communications may be retained while we deal with your enquiry and for a reasonable period afterwards for record-keeping and dispute resolution.

When information is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to delete, de-identify, or anonymise it where this is feasible.

11. Security

We take appropriate, reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against loss, unauthorised access, and unlawful processing. These measures include the use of reputable hosting providers, access controls, software updates, secure configuration, and moderation controls.
