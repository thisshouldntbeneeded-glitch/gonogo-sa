What it is

A public trust signal with a private performance layer behind it.

The public side of GoNoGo is the score your brand can display on-site, in campaigns, and across customer touchpoints. The private side is the workspace your team uses to understand what is driving that score, what themes keep appearing in customer sentiment, and which actions are most likely to move performance in the right direction.

That is what makes GoNoGo Business more than a badge. It turns a static score into a more useful operating signal — one that supports better prioritisation, more confident decisions, and more consistent action over time.
