How business participation works

GoNoGo helps brands understand the drivers behind their public score, gather customer feedback, respond to reviews, and improve trust through transparent engagement. GoNoGo's tools are designed to help brands act on insight and communicate with customers more effectively, while preserving the independence of GoNoGo's ratings and review systems.

Brands can claim and manage their profile, reply publicly where appropriate, invite customer feedback through GoNoGo tools, and display approved GoNoGo widgets or badges where eligible. However, commercial participation does not guarantee a particular score, ranking, badge status, review outcome, or editorial position.

No pay-to-play. GoNoGo does not sell higher scores, better rankings, removal of genuine reviews, or favourable editorial treatment. Payment for business tools, profile services, widgets, data access, or badge licences does not affect the independence of GoNoGo's scores or reviews.

Claiming a profile lets an authorised representative verify their connection to the brand, respond to reviews, access business tools, and manage certain factual profile details. Claiming a profile does not transfer ownership of the page, does not give the brand control over GoNoGo's editorial content or scoring, and does not guarantee any score, ranking, badge or review outcome.
