What brands get

GoNoGo gives businesses a simple way to display trust publicly and understand customer experience privately — without building a whole research or feedback stack from scratch.

GoNoGo score badge

Show your independent GoNoGo score on your homepage, landing pages or campaigns with a live badge that updates automatically.

  • Full, compact or score-only styles.
  • Simple copy-paste embed.
  • Links back to your GoNoGo profile.

Private business portal

Access your score, verdict, framework breakdown, strengths and concerns in one clear dashboard your marketing or CX team can use immediately.

  • View score breakdowns by category.
  • See strengths and key concerns clearly.
  • Use your data in reports and presentations.

QR feedback collection

Put QR codes in stores, airports, hospitals, service desks or venues so customers can rate the experience while they’re still there.

  • Capture fresh comments on-site.
  • Spot patterns faster.
  • Turn feedback into practical action.
