How We Score

Every brand is evaluated on a 100-point framework across multiple weighted categories

Compliance & Regulation

Licensing, regulatory standing, industry body memberships, and legal compliance within the South African market.

Customer Satisfaction

Real user feedback from app stores, review platforms, social media sentiment, and complaint resolution rates.

Product Value

Pricing fairness, feature breadth, transparency of fees, and overall value for money in the SA context.

Innovation

Technology adoption, digital experience quality, unique differentiators, and forward-thinking approach.

Customer Support

Availability of support channels, response times, resolution quality, and accessibility of help resources.

Accessibility & Security

Platform accessibility, data security practices, POPIA compliance, and inclusive design considerations.

Note: Each industry has its own specific scoring categories with tailored weightings. The categories above represent the general framework.
