      What works best

      Use concise surveys and distribute them close to the customer moment you want feedback on.

      • Ask at the right moment
        Send post-purchase surveys within 24 hours, support surveys right after resolution, and QR surveys at point of service.
      • Keep it short
        Five to eight well-phrased questions usually outperform long forms and protect completion rates.
      • Always include one open question
        The rating tells you what happened; the comment tells you why it happened.
