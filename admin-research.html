About Data Freshness

This page tracks when each brand's data was last reviewed:

  • Fresh (green) — data reviewed within 14 days
  • Stale (orange) — data is 15-30 days old, should be checked
  • Outdated (red) — data is over 30 days old, needs attention

Click Mark Fresh on a brand after you've manually verified its data is current. Use Refresh Stale or Auto-Refresh Outdated to batch-mark brands as reviewed.
