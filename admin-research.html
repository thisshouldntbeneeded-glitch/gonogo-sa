How Auto-Research Works

When you click Refresh on a brand, the system automatically:

  1. Checks the Google Play Store for current app rating
  2. Queries the Apple App Store (iTunes API) for iOS rating
  3. Looks up Hellopeter trust index and review count
  4. Verifies website accessibility and HTTPS status
  5. Updates the brand's Last Researched date

Updated data syncs to Google Sheets via the daily auto-sync. Use Auto-Research Outdated to refresh all brands over 30 days old (up to 10 at a time).
